the brand

Award-winning golf software

One Iota Golf Performance is an award-winning Vancouver-based start up that was co-founded by SFU's Ryan Stols. Their browser-based product is the only golf statistics tracking software to incorporate the usage of mental statistics. These statistics are then used by golfers of all skill levels to track progress and compare themselves with others.

project overview

general problems

why was a design intervention needed?

Ryan came to the Hackathon with a clear goal of having a mobile app designed due a number of reasons, as uncovered through our client interview:

  • current software is desktop-only
  • novice users are overwhelmed with the complexity and amount of statistics involved
  • novice users are unsure of how to navigate the software
  • there is a lack of integration between the mental and quantitative golf statistics

solution

our designs

We addressed the problem of users not knowing how to use the software by creating an onboarding experience. This also allows users to customize their preferences to allow for a personalized experience.
